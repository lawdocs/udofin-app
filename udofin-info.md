# Udofin Context & Information

## What is Udofin?
Udofin is a technology platform built for fair, transparent, and borrower-first digital lending. It is a premium fintech lending platform and operates as a **Lending Service Provider (LSP)** registered under the Reserve Bank of India (Digital Lending) Directions, 2025.

Udofin operates a multi-lender marketplace that allows users to submit a single loan application and have it evaluated against the eligibility and pricing criteria of multiple RBI-regulated banks and non-banking financial companies (NBFCs).

## Core Philosophy & Operations
- **Unbiased Matching**: Requests are routed to matched NBFCs without bias or hidden incentives. Offers are ranked based on borrower-relevant criteria—never on which NBFC pays Udofin more.
- **No Fund Pooling**: Udofin operates a strict "No Fund Pooling" policy. Loan disbursements flow directly from the Partner Lender to the borrower's verified bank account, and repayments flow directly back to the lender. Udofin never touches the loan funds or lends on its own book.
- **Free for Borrowers**: Udofin does not charge borrowers a fee for using the matching and comparison service (unless expressly disclosed at the time of application).

## Key Features & Perks
- **Smart Loan Advisor ("Fin")**: An intelligent, professional, and friendly AI Loan Advisor that assists users in the application process.
- **Speed & Convenience**: Offers a "10-second eligibility check" and facilitates "Instant disbursal."
- **Safe Evaluation**: Applying through Udofin has "No credit score impact."
- **Transparency**: Ditching complex terms and hidden fees for a frictionless borrowing experience.

## Security & Compliance
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Strictly complies with the **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023** and RBI guidelines.
- **Data Privacy**: 
  - Access to user data is strictly role-based and logged. 
  - Udofin **never** accesses contacts, call logs, or personal files.
  - Users can view, manage, and revoke data access directly from their dashboard.
